## Design Context

**Register:** product (design serves the product — dashboards, tools, app UI)
**Brand Voice:** Warm, Bold, Trustworthy
**Creative North Star:** The Baobab Canopy

### Design Principles
1. **Warm authority** — Friendly but serious. Approachable like a great teacher, not a robot.
2. **African-first** — Designed for African learners — mobile-conscious, accessible, culturally relevant.
3. **Show, don't tell** — Visual feedback, progress indicators, data viz over walls of text.
4. **Practice what you preach** — The product teaches; the UI itself should be well-crafted and instructive.

### Key Visual Rules
- **Burnt Orange** (`#ff7000`) on ≤10% of any screen — its rarity is its power
- Warm tonal layering for depth, not heavy shadows
- No cool grays, no pure black/white, no corporate LMS aesthetic
- Vary card styles — no identical repeated patterns
- Space Grotesk for headings, Geist for body — never swap them
