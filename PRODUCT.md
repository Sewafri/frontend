# Product

## Register

product

## Users

**Students** — African learners accessing courses, tracking progress, managing their learning journey. Often mobile-first, with varying connectivity and device quality.

**Instructors** — Subject matter experts creating courses, managing content, tracking student performance.

**Admins** — Platform operators managing users, courses, payments, certificates, analytics, and applications.

## Product Purpose

SewAfri makes African education accessible, trackable, and administrable — one platform where students learn, instructors teach, and operators run. Success looks like students completing courses, instructors growing their reach, and the platform scaling across the continent.

## Brand Personality

**Warm. Bold. Trustworthy.**

Voice is friendly but serious — approachable like a great teacher, not a corporate LMS. The brand radiates confidence without arrogance, warmth without saccharine. Emotional goals: pride in progress, trust in the platform, excitement about learning.

## Anti-references

- **Generic corporate edtech** — Not Blackboard, Moodle, Coursera, or any cookie-cutter LMS. No rigid card grids, sterile blues, or dense table UI.
- **AI-generated look** — No identical cards, templated gradients, stale symmetry, or the telltale signs of generative UI. Every surface should feel deliberately placed by a human designer.

## Design Principles

1. **Warm authority** — Friendly but serious. Approachable like a great teacher, not a robot. The UI earns trust through polish: intentional spacing, deliberate color, clear hierarchy.

2. **African-first** — Designed for African learners' context. Mobile-conscious, connectivity-aware, culturally relevant. Not a western platform with African content bolted on.

3. **Show, don't tell** — Progress indicators, data visualization, visual feedback over walls of text. A student should feel their progress, not read about it.

4. **Practice what you preach** — The product teaches; the UI itself should be well-crafted and instructive. Well-organized information, clear affordances, thoughtful micro-interactions demonstrate craft by example.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target WCAG 2.2 AA
- Dark/light mode with sufficient contrast in both
- Reduced motion support via Tailwind's `motion-reduce:` utilities
- Focus-visible indicators on all interactive elements
- Mobile-first responsive design for varying device quality and connectivity
